Number Game

Game Rules:

  • Click on start game button.
  • You get 3 random numbers b/w 1 to 9 in input boxes

Game Process:

  • If you get all 3 same numbers then your score will get 1000 points.
  • If you get all 3 numbers as either even or odd, then you will get 300 points.
  • If you get the 3 numbers with difference of 1 you will get 800 points.
  • If none of the above conditions are met you will get 0 points.You can not continue the game.Need to start again.
  • For each next round click on button "Click Me to update numbers". and Your remaining score will subtract with 100.
  • Continue till you get remaining score less than or equal to 0.
  • End of Game
